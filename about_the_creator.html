About the creator

Hi, I am Kasak! This project began as a simple idea to create a space where ancient myths meet personal identity.
Every part of Realm within from visuals to interactions was built by me as a part of learning design and development.
I wanted to craft something that feels different from a typical website. something with personality, emotion and soul.
Though rooted in greek mythology realm within isn’t about belief or worship, it’s about exploring timeless stories and seeing how they reflect different parts of who we are the strength, fear, intution,resilence.
